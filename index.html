The Capitol Hill Insider Guide

Location benefits as neighborhood secrets

Forget what you know about apartment living. At 1270 Logan St, you're not just renting a place - you're claiming your spot in Denver's most authentic neighborhood.

Capitol Hill isn't just walkable - it's the most walkable neighborhood in Denver. And we're right at its creative heart. Step out your door and you're blocks from the 13th Avenue scene that defines Denver's food, art, and nightlife culture.

With Cheesman Park just a stone's throw away and the State Capitol within view, you're living where Denver's civic and social life intersect. Plus, downtown access without the downtown rent premium.

13th Avenue

3 min walk

The beating heart of Cap Hill. Dive bars, late-night tacos, and Denver's best people-watching. Start at Charlie's and end up... somewhere.

Cheesman Park

8 min walk

30 acres of green space built on an old cemetery (very goth, very Cap Hill). Perfect for sunset picnics or pretending to exercise.

Downtown

0.8 mi

Close enough to walk when you need to adult. Far enough away that you don't have to deal with the Union Station tourists.

Colfax Nightlife

5 min

The longest, wickedest street in America is your backyard. From Bluebird Theater to 1UP Arcade Bar, your evenings just got interesting.

Whole Foods

10 min walk

For when you need to adult-adult. Also King Soopers at 9th & Corona when your bank account says "maybe next week."

State Capitol

4 blocks

Watch democracy in action (or inaction). Great spot for protests, festivals, or just sitting on the steps feeling important.

Food Scene

less than 5 blocks

100+ restaurants, bars, and cafes within walking distance. From hole-in-the-wall gems to trendy spots that'll make your Instagram jealous.
